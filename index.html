Informatikk: Programmering og Systemarkitektur

The bachelor's program in Informatikk: programmering og systemarkitektur at UiO (University of Oslo) is designed to give you a deep understanding of how software and computer systems are built.

Throughout this program, you learn how to develop large, complex IT systems, understand how computers work under the hood, and write efficient, robust code. It covers everything from basic object-oriented programming to advanced system design, logic, and algorithms.
